About

I am a PhD researcher at the Faculty of Aquatic Sciences, Istanbul University. My research interests include seafood processing technologies, seafood safety, environmental contamination, and microplastic risks in seafood. I work at the intersection of environmental health and aquatic food systems, with a strong focus on risk assessment, contaminant bioaccumulation, and quality indicators in processed seafood. I actively contribute to multidisciplinary scientific projects and publish extensively in SCI-Expanded journals.
